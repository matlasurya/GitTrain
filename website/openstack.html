OpenStack Training

Course Overview

We offer two day’s course on OpenStack Cloud Fundamentals. It includes Understanding, Installing and configuring all the OpenStack services. The classes provide hands-on training with real situations in a training lab environment.

By the end of the OpenStack Course, learners will be able to

  • Understand the architecture of an OpenStack Cloud deployment.
  • Learning the key features of OpenStack
  • Understanding suitable use-cases for OpenStack
  • Deploy an OpenStack multi-server environment
  • Implement and use OpenStack Image Service, OpenStack Identity, and OpenStack Dashboard services
  • Create and manage images and instances
  • Create and manage roles, users, and quotas
  • Troubleshoot common issues in an OpenStack environment

Who should take OpenStack training?

System administrators and solution archite.cts who will be deploying OpenStack clouds are ideal for the training courses.

What benefits does this course give me?

Students will gain hands-on experience on OpenStack Compute Cloud. They will be able to Configure and Deploy OpenStack in real-world environment.
